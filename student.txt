# Attributes for both student-mat.csv (Math course) dataset:
Binary yes->1 no->0
1 age - student's age (numeric: from 15 to 22)
2 Pstatus - parent's cohabitation status (binary: "T/1" - living together or "A/0" - apart)
3 Medu - mother's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education)
4 Fedu - father's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education)
5 traveltime - home to school travel time (numeric: 1 - <15 min., 2 - 15 to 30 min., 3 - 30 min. to 1 hour, or 4 - >1 hour)
6 studytime - weekly study time (numeric: 1 - <2 hours, 2 - 2 to 5 hours, 3 - 5 to 10 hours, or 4 - >10 hours)
7 schoolsup - extra educational support (binary: yes or no)
8 famsup - family educational support (binary: yes or no)
9 activities - extra-curricular activities (binary: yes or no)
10 higher - wants to take higher education (binary: yes or no)
11 romantic - with a romantic relationship (binary: yes or no)
12 Walc - weekend alcohol consumption (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
13 health - current health status (numeric: from 1 - very bad to 5 - very good)
14 absences - number of school absences (numeric: from 0 to 93)

# these grades are related with the course subject, Math:
15 G3 - final grade (numeric: from 0 to 20, output target)
